BioGene is an information tool for biological research. Use BioGene to learn about gene function.

Enter a gene symbol or gene name, for example "CDK4" or "cyclin dependent kinase 4" and hit SEARCH.

BioGene was produced with the Computational Biology Center at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center and Dana-Farber Cancer Institute using content from Entrez Gene at the NCBI. Send us your feedback.
